Oasis Coffee

Fall 2022

Materials created for a fictional brand I named "Oasis Coffee", including a logo, brand guide, and promotional booklet. The logo was created in Adobe Illustrator, the branding guide was created in Adobe Photoshop, and the promotional booklet was put together using Adobe InDesign.

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Hatch Style Poster

Fall 2022

Concert poster created to look like a hatch show print for the band Bones UK. This was for a fictionalized concert but based on a real band. Everything was done digitally but meant to look like it was from a traditional letterpress shop.

Adobe Photoshop

Horror Movie Poster

Fall 2022

A poster for a fictional horror movie I called A Day to Dismember, heavily inspired by the song "Wurst Vacation" from the band Ice Nine Kills, which is in turn inspired by the Hostel franchise. I wanted to come up with something that would invoke similar feelings to Hostel or Saw while still giving it my own flair.

Adobe Photoshop

Typeface + Type Specimen Poster

I created a typeface I named "It's a Sign", meant to mimic traditional sign painting. This was drawn in Procreate then taken to Photoshop to create the complete type specimen poster.
